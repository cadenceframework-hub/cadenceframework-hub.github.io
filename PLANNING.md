Cadence Sync
Every client, regardless of industry, goes through these four phases of the Cadence Framework:
- The Audit (Pulse Check): The "Set up a call" phase. We map their current workflow, identifying where they are "stuck in maintenance." 
- The Blueprint (The Score): We design the step-by-step process. The workflow map of how your services (AI, Web, Content) will talk to each other + what isn't working, what is 
- The Integration (The Downbeat): This is the "Build Up" phase. We implement the UI/UX, the AI automations, and the Brand Identity, which you need based on our services. 
- The Momentum (The Resonance): The things we build - we will now make sure the systems are running, and that the company can actually "do the work" while your systems handle the growth - so you can focus on what is needed - not running the company but leading it (maintenance to movement)
